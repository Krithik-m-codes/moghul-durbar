About Us

Since 1969, Moghul Durbar has been a cherished destination for authentic South Indian cuisine. Nestled in a historic setting, our restaurant is a celebration of the rich flavors and culinary traditions of the region.
With a commitment to quality and a passion for South Indian gastronomy, we invite you to savor the essence of tradition in every bite.

Join us on a journey where history meets flavor

and indulge in the timeless charm of Moghul Durbar.
